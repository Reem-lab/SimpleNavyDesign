ABOUT US

Established in 1983, ETMS is one of the first private maritime companies in Egypt. We started as a ship agency at all Egyptian ports and Suez Canal representing and serving owners of reefer livestock, bulk, general cargo, and heavy-lift vessels.

In 1987, the company became very active in time-chartering as well as booking vessel spaces for special projects; IMO, bulk, and reefer cargo, serving mainly the North Continent Med trade.

From 1990 to 2015, we commercially operated a variety of ships, and fully managed others in both tramping and semi-liner trades. The ships varied between general cargo, MPP, RORO, and reefer types.

During this period, the agency department has vastly grown to be offering a wide range of services including cargo / operations survey, stevedoring, storage, and cargo handling at all local ports, specializing in heavy projects and dangerous goods.
